Effective Design

What's It For?

It's for you. Effective Design is a service (currently only existing in my brain) that gives you a way to make sure your materials, whether for print or screen, are as successful as they can be.

Why Bother?

As a college student, I see lots of fliers advertising various clubs, events, and other things. But almost all of these posters have poor grammar and unappealing design. I want to help with that, so I had a thought: “Would people pay a couple bucks to have someone professionally proofread their paper? Would a club (or church group) think about using a designer to make their poster more eye-catching?” Sure.

So why not have a simple, inexpensive design company? I love the idea. Do you? Send me an email, long or short, to tyler.wayne.dev@gmail.com letting me know what you think. As long as I'm not the only one in favor of the concept, I'll get it moving.
